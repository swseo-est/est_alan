RISK MANAGEMENT FRAMEWORK

Key Risk Categories: Our framework identifies and addresses four primary risk categories that could impact our growth strategy.

Market Risks: Competitive disruption, demand shifts, pricing pressure

Operational Risks: Supply chain disruption, talent shortage, technology failures

Financial Risks: Exchange rate volatility, capital constraints, credit exposure

Compliance Risks: Regulatory changes, legal liabilities, governance issues

Risk Management Approach:

  • Proactive identification and continuous monitoring
  • Quantitative impact assessment methodology
  • Cross-functional risk mitigation taskforces
  • Quarterly risk review with executive leadership
